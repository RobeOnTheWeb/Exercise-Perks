What Is Exercise Physiology?

Exercise Physiology aims to help individuals improve their quality of life, maintain their independence and prevent and/or manage chronic conditions/injuries through safe and individualised physical activity.

Exercise Physiologist undertake a 4 year University degree to be able to work as an Allied Health Professional. With an extensive knowledge in chronic health conditions and injuries Exercise Physiologists are able to prescribe safe and effect exercises to those who may have cardiovascular, respiratory, metabolic (eg diabetes), neurological, mental health, musculoskeletal and other conditions.

As an Exercise Physiologist we believe that Exercise has the power to act as a powerful medication that can help treat a wide range of conditions if appropriately prescribed!
