Services

Initial Assessment

45 minutes

Everyone who attends Exercise Perks requires an Initial Assessment. During this consultation Georgia will discuss with you your medical history, complete a couple of assessments, provide education about how physical activity may benefit you, discuss some goals and a suitable plan moving forward.

Standard Session

45 minutes

This session will vary depending on the individual and will usually last between 30- 45minutes. A standard consult may be a completing a gym-based exercise program, going through a home program, completing a hydrotherapy session, providing education or reviewing your current program. All programs will be individually tailored to work towards specific goals.

Group Session

1 hour

A fun, welcoming and affordable way to develop a regular exercise routine. Exercise Perks offers a range of group class times, lasting for 1 hour each. Each participant will compete their own individualised exercises a group setting tailored to their own goals. Sessions will be safe with the supervision of Georgia at all times.

Home Visit

45 minutes

If you can't come to us, perhaps we can come to you and complete exercise in the comfort of your own home!
