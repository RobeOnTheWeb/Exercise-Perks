About

Exercise Perks offers a variety of different exercise environments including home visits, development of home programs, small group classes and one and one sessions to suit everyone’s needs. We understand that "exercise" is not for everyone, so our job is to try and find some sort of movement that is manageable and enjoyable for each individual. At Exercise Perks we encourage long term lifestyle changes by equipping clients with the tools and education to manage their own health and well being.

Georgia returned to Robe in 2019 to establish Exercise Perks after working and studying in Adelaide for six years. Georgia is passionate about sharing her knowledge and skills to help strive for a healthier community. Georgia has really enjoyed returning home to work with and meet so many great community members within the South East region.

Where possible Georgia tries to make exercise as enjoyable and meaningful as possible, trying to find some sort of exercise for everyone. Georgia has a large focus on completing exercises that are somewhat functional to daily tasks, aiming to make the easier things in life easier such as holding your grandkids or putting on your socks.

Georgia works closely with the General Practitioners and other health professionals in the area such as Physiotherapists, Podiatrists , Chiropractors and Dietitians to ensure the most appropriate management plan is developed for her clients.

Who can exercise Benefit:

Exercise Perks helps to manage and prevent a range of conditions, including but not limited to:

- Cardiovascular conditions
- Respiratory conditions
- Mental health conditions
- Arthritis
- Back pain
- Type 1 and 2 Diabetes
- Developmental conditions/disabilities
- Osteoporosis
- Pre and Post surgery
- Cancer recovery
- Pre and Post Pregnancy
- Parkinson’s Disease
- Muscle strengthening
- Balance
- Chronic Pain.

Exercise can also help to improve your strength, balance & prolong your independence.
